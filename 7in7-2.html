Nocturne

Due Date: Oct,23 2020

Introducion

Nocturne is a audio work I created at 3:00am, just the subjective expression of my feelings when staying up really late. The melody of this audio is relatively stable, kind of hypnotic, but not relaxing at all.

Inspiration

When I stay up late, my mental state is usually chaotic, half asleep and half awake, this audio is based on my blurred consciousness, so the melody is mostly formed by procrastinating long syllables.
